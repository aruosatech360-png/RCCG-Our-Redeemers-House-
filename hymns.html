Hymns of the Month

Join us in lifting our voices in praise.

I’ve a message from the Lord, Hallelujah!
The message unto you I’ll give,
’Tis recorded in His word, Hallelujah!
It is only that you “look and live.”

Chorus:
“Look and live,” my brother, live,
Look to Jesus now and live;
’Tis recorded in His word, Hallelujah!
It is only that you “look and live.”

I’ve a message full of love, Hallelujah!
A message, O my friend, for you,
’Tis a message from above, Hallelujah!
Jesus said it and I know ’tis true.

Life is offered unto you, Hallelujah!
Eternal life thy soul shall have,
If you’ll only look to Him, Hallelujah!
Look to Jesus who alone can save.

We will soon be done with troubles and trials,
Yes, we'll soon be done with troubles and trials,
In that home on the other side.
I'm going home to live with Jesus.

Chorus:
We will soon shout Hallelujah, Hallelujah, Hallelujah,
We will soon shout Hallelujah,
In that home on the other side.

No more weeping and a-wailing,
No more weeping and a-wailing,
In that home on the other side.
I'm going home to live with Jesus.

“Showers of blessing,” we hear the glad sound;
Mercy-drops round us are falling;
But for the showers we plead, O Lord, now,
Send them upon us, O Lord.

Chorus:
Showers of blessing,
Showers of blessing we need;
Mercy-drops round us are falling,
But for the showers we plead.

“Showers of blessing,” so great is our need;
Thou, O our God, art the Giver;
Thou dost refresh and revive us indeed;
Send them upon us, O Lord.

I need Thee every hour, most gracious Lord;
No tender voice like Thine can peace afford.

Chorus:
I need Thee, O I need Thee;
Every hour I need Thee;
O bless me now, my Savior, I come to Thee.

I need Thee every hour, stay Thou nearby;
Temptations lose their power when Thou art nigh.

I need Thee every hour, in joy or pain;
Come quickly and abide, or life is vain.
