Thanksgiving Service

Join us every first Sunday of the month for our special Thanksgiving service, where we come together to praise, rejoice, and dance in gratitude for God's faithfulness and mercies. Experience uplifting moments including birthday celebrations, special thanksgivings, tea time networking, and natural group meetings. Don't miss out on this unforgettable day!

Venue: Main Church Auditorium

Marriage in RCCG

In The Redeemed Christian Church of God, we view marriage as a sacred covenant ordained by God. It is a lifelong commitment between a man and a woman, reflecting Christ's relationship with the Church. We provide comprehensive pre-marital counseling to prepare couples for this blessed journey, focusing on spiritual unity, communication, and shared values. For more information, you can download our marriage guide or contact us to make an enquiry.
