A Warm Welcome From Our Pastor

It is with great joy that I welcome you to the online home of RCCG Our Redeemer's House. Whether you are a cherished member of our family or a first-time visitor exploring your faith, we are so glad you're here.

Our Redeemer's House is a vibrant community, a spiritual home for many young families dedicated to growing in God's love and purpose. We are committed to building a foundation of faith through heartfelt worship, genuine fellowship, and dedicated service.

I encourage you to explore our website to discover more about our ministries and upcoming events. Better yet, we would be honored to have you join us for one of our services. You are always welcome here.

Pastor Lanre Akinyo

Pastor-in-Charge, Our Redeemer's House
